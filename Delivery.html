Delivery Information

Home-Delivery Information

1. Shipping Methods

We partner with trusted courier services to deliver your orders safely and on time. Depending on your location and the products ordered, we offer:

  • Standard Delivery
  • Express Delivery
  • Same-Day Delivery (available in select areas)

2. Delivery Times

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected:

  • Standard Delivery: 3–7 business days
  • Express Delivery: 1–3 business days
  • Same-Day Delivery: Orders placed before 12 PM in eligible areas

Please note that delivery times may be affected by holidays, high demand periods, or unforeseen courier delays.

3. Delivery Costs

Delivery costs depend on your location, the size/weight of your order, and the selected shipping method. The total delivery fee will be calculated at checkout.

Free delivery may be available for orders above a certain value, as indicated in our promotions.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link so you can monitor your delivery in real-time.

5. International Delivery

We ship to multiple countries worldwide. International delivery times vary between 7–21 business days depending on the destination and customs processing.

Customers are responsible for any applicable customs duties, taxes, or import fees.
